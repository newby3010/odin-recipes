Shepherd Pie

This is the Cabbage

The beautiful thing about this dish it is simlple to do , all you need is the patience to do it. As allways made by yours truly Kevin.

Ingredients

  • Make mash potato's
  • Boil and mash carrots
  • Cook beef-onion mixture
  • Assemble the shepherd pie
  • Bake the shepherd pie until the top is golden brown


Steps

  1. 4 large potatos
  2. 1 tbl butter
  3. 1/4 cup cherddar cheese
  4. 5 carrots
  5. 1 tbl vegetable oil
  6. 1 onion choped
  7. 1 pound of beef
  8. 2 tbl all-purpose flour
  9. 1 tbl ketchup
  10. 3/4 cup beef broth
  11. 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese
